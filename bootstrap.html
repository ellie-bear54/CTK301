Birds of Paradise

Birds of paradise reside in dense rainforests in eastern Indonesia, Papua New Guinea, and eastern Australia. They are best known for their impressive plumage, which is typically seen on the male birds of paradise. They primarily eat fruit, though it is not uncommon to see arthropods as a part of their diet.
