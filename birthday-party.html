Livin' La Vida Loca topped the charts from May 8-June 11, 1999

"Livin' La Vida Loca," recorded by Ricky Martin and written by Draco Rosa and Desmond Child, was released by Columbia Records on March 23, 1999. It was released as the lead single off the album "Ricky Martin," Martin's fifth studio album and English-language debut.

In 1999, the music video received nominations for "Maximum Vision Award" and "Best New Artist Clip - Dance" from the Billboard Music Video Awards; "Video of the Year," "Best Male Video," "Best Choreography," and "Viewer's Choice" in the MTV Video Music Awards, winning "Best Dance Video" and "Best Pop Video," as well as International Viewer's Choice Awards in North America, South America, and Russia.
